In my opinion, despite the writings of many others on the subject, divination is not passive. In the same way that a work of art is a dialogue between the artist and the beholder, a reading is a dialogue between spiritual forces and the reader. Consequently, even though I use somewhat different interpretations than other authors do, this isn't a problem: as long as the spirits around you are aware of and understand your interpretative methods, it should work out fine. (This is also why "finishing what you start" is such a critical dictum, karma aside: if you bounce around, you become unpredictable, and it is difficult for others—spirit or not—to perceive how you will react, and you can't build up a relationship with your tools and processes.)
