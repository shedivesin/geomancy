The elements are usually associated with hot+dry, hot+wet, cold+wet, cold+dry, following Aristotle. I think this is a mistake; doing so causes a severe asymmetry: air is day+day, and therefore should go above fire (which is day+night), but this would destroy the symbolism of the elements. Further, we know empirically that more subtle than air in practice.
